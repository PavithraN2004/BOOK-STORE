JOHN GREEN



John Michael Green is the author of Looking for Alaska, An Abundance of Katherines, Paper Towns, and The Fault in Our Stars. He is an American author, vlogger, writer, producer, actor and editor. He was born in Indianapolis.His sixth book, The Fault in Our Stars, was released in January 2012. It debuted at number one on The New York Times Best Seller list.
